## Ideological Clusters

### Safety-First Cluster
**Core:** Stuart Russell, Eliezer Yudkowsky, Tristan Harris
**Characteristics:** Strong safety advocacy, regulatory interventionism, skeptical of rapid deployment
**Influence:** Policy, academic safety research, EA funding

### Scaling Optimists
**Core:** Demis Hassabis, Lex Fridman, Kai-Fu Lee
**Characteristics:** Believe scaling delivers capabilities, support continued development
**Influence:** Research direction, investment, public understanding

### Pragmatic Practitioners
**Core:** Cassie Kozyrkov, Mustafa Suleyman
**Characteristics:** Bridge technical and business/policy, emphasize practical deployment
**Influence:** Enterprise adoption, governance frameworks

### Critical Humanists
**Core:** Jaron Lanier, Tristan Harris
**Characteristics:** Question economic models and social impacts, advocate for human dignity
**Influence:** Data rights, labor economics, democratic discourse

### Geopolitical Realists
**Core:** Kai-Fu Lee
**Characteristics:** Frame AI as competitive race, prioritize national advantage
**Influence:** Asian strategy, innovation-first policies
